Bologna Cake

Description

You may think that this is a sweet cake for dessert but I'm sorry to inform you that it's not, this Baloney Cake (also known as Bologna Cake) is a simple, savory Southern dish, popularly served as an appetizer or side dish at family events. Whether you are curious or just looking for this Baloney cake recipe, you should give this cake a try, it's really worth it.

If you have a large group of people that you need to feed, or you are just confused and have no idea what to serve as an appetizer or side dish, no need for further looking this Baloney / Bologna cake will make the job done. Baloney / Bologna cake is just too simple to make and has only 3 simple ingredients plus an optional cheese whiz for garnish, it can be prepared In less than 20 minutes, and will end up with a tasty appetizer and a conversation starter.

Ingredients

  • Bologna
  • Cream Cheese
  • Onion Powder
  • Cheese Whiz

Steps

  1. Take a large bowl and add into it cream cheese(need to be at room temperature ) and onion powder, then mix them together using a spatula.
  2. Now, you want to take one slice of bologna from the package and pat dry it with paper towels, then place it on a plate.
  3. After that, with the use of an offset spatula go ahead and spread a layer of cream cheese mixture on top of the bologna.
  4. Next, spread about 1 tablespoon of cream cheese mixture between each layer, and keep repeating the process until you run out of bologna slices.
  5. Then you want to spread more cream cheese mixture onto the side of the bologna cake.
  6. For more decoration to your bologna cake, you can cut the corner of a piping bag or zip-top bag, then push a decorating nozzle right into the hole.
  7. Finally, Fill it with some cheese whiz, then push the cheese onto your cake, serve it and enjoy!
